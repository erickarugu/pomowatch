What's Pomowatch

Pomowatch is a lightweight, easy to use, customizable pomodoro technique timer to help you with tracking your time using the famous pomodor technique for maximum productivity.

We believe in making every minute of every day count. Whether working, working out or perfoming any regular task.

What is pomodoro technique

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. The technique uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks of 5 minutes and long breaks of 10 minutes at regular intervals.
